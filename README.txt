SharpTools is the ultimate app for your smart home. It lets you create stunning dashboards that show off your smart devices and make them easy to control. 

You can customize every tile to match your style and mood, and add tiles for the things that matter to you. Whether you want to dim the lights, crank up the heat, rock out to your favorite tunes, or keep an eye on your furry friends, you can do it all with a simple touch or swipe on your dashboard. 

You can access your dashboards from any device with a web browser, so you can always stay connected to your smart home. You can also share your dashboards with your loved ones or impress your guests. 

With SharpTools, you can control and monitor your smart home with ease and style.
